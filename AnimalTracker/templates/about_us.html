About Us

Welcome to Animal Tracker! We understand the importance of pets and livestock in your life. Whether they provide companionship, emotional support, or are crucial to your agricultural business, ensuring their safety and security is paramount.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide a reliable solution for pet owners and farmers to track and locate their animals efficiently. Our GPS-based tracking system offers real-time location data, helping you monitor your animals and act quickly if they stray or get lost.

Key Features

  • Real-time location updates
  • Alerts when animals leave designated areas
  • Allows users to change safe zone

With Animal Tracker, you can reduce search time, enhance security against theft and loss, and improve overall animal management. Access the system anytime and anywhere with an internet connection.
